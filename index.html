About Online Voting

Online is a modern approach to elections, allowing voters to cast their ballots securely over the internet.It eliminates the need of physical polling stations,reduces cost and enhance accessibility.

Why Online Voting Matters

  • Convinience: vote from anywhere, anytime
  • Security: Advanced encryption and authentication methods ensure safety
  • Transparency: Real-time tracking and audit logs for accountability
  • Inclusion: Provides easier access for people with dissabilites and those in remote areas
  • Faster Results: Automated counting speeds up election processes
